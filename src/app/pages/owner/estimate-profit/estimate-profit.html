Platform Charges & Fees

Transparent Pricing
No Hidden Charges

A small percentage of each booking keeps the platform secure, reliable, and supported. Below is a transparent breakdown of the fees and ongoing owner costs.

Platform Charges - 15%

Car2Go charges a 15% service fee from total rental revenue for using the platform, managing bookings, and providing support services.

GST on Platform Fee - 18%

As per government regulations, 18% GST is applied to the platform service fee.

Maintenance Cost

Regular servicing, repairs, and cleaning keep the vehicle in top condition.

Insurance Cost

Owners are responsible for maintaining valid vehicle insurance as part of operating costs.
